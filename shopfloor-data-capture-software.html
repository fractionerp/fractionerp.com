Shopfloor Data Capture Software

Capture real-time production data directly from your factory floor. Fraction ERP's shopfloor data capture tools give you live visibility of job progress, operator activity, and machine performance — without the paperwork.

Whether your team is clocking on to jobs, recording completions, or flagging issues, everything is updated instantly across the system. No more chasing the shop floor for updates.

Live Production Visibility

See exactly what is happening on your shop floor, in real time.

  • Track job start and finish times automatically
  • Monitor operator and work centre activity
  • View live progress against your production schedule
  • Reduce time spent chasing updates from the factory floor

Better Decisions, Faster

With accurate, up-to-date information flowing from the shop floor into your ERP system, managers can make faster decisions about scheduling, capacity, and delivery commitments. No more guesswork, no more surprises.

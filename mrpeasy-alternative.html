Key Differences

1

Simplicity vs Feature Density

Fraction ERP is built around usability. Manage quotes, jobs, purchasing, and production without complex menus.

MRPeasy offers more features in some areas, but this can come at the cost of usability — especially for teams without dedicated ERP specialists.

2

Implementation & Onboarding

Fraction ERP gets you live quickly, often without consultants. Onboard your team within days.

MRPeasy follows a self-service model. Documentation is strong, but businesses often need more time to configure workflows.

3

Support Experience

With Fraction ERP, support is part of the product. Direct access to people who understand manufacturing.

MRPeasy relies more on structured support channels, which can feel less hands-on for businesses needing guidance.

4

Flexibility for Real-World Manufacturing

Fraction ERP is well suited to make-to-order, job-based, and engineering-led businesses.

MRPeasy is more standardised, which works well for repeatable production but can feel rigid for complex workflows.

When to Choose Each System

Choose Fraction ERP if you:

  • Want to go live quickly
  • Value simplicity and clarity
  • Need hands-on support
  • Run a job-based or engineering-led operation

Choose MRPeasy if you:

  • Need advanced production scheduling tools
  • Prefer an all-in-one system with built-in CRM/accounting
  • Have time to configure and manage internally

Why Companies Look for MRPeasy Alternatives

Many manufacturers start with MRPeasy, but later look for alternatives when:

  • The system becomes harder to manage as operations grow
  • Teams struggle with usability or training
  • More flexible workflows are needed
  • They want more direct support

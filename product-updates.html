Product Updates

Keep up to date with Fraction ERP development! All updates are automatically rolled out.

12/2025
WO Quantity Tracking at Operations

The tracking of work order is enhanced by capturing how many parts are processed through each operation, this is done by the SFDC user updating how many have been completed.

12/2025
Export KPI Data

All KPIs now feature a download option to get access to the data behind all of your company's KPIs.

12/2025
Company Holidays

Specify the dates your company will be closed and these are set as non working days in the production scheduling tool.

11/2025
Enhanced copy Bill of Materials

Users can now copy a bill of materials and choose to copy parts, operations or both to the new BOM record.

11/2025
Subcontract Operations

Manage subcontract operations by specifying how many days require for subcontract and this is built into the production schedule.

10/2025
Lot Pricing and Lot Quantities

Enhancing the lot feature to allow pricing per lot on sales orders whilst ensuring production quantities follow total units, not total lots.

09/2025
Plastic Packaging Tax Report for Plastic Injection Moulding

A new report developed for plastic packaging tax for use in our moulding plans to calculate tax from all parts that require plastic packaging tax.

08/2025
Custom Workcentre List in Drag and Drop Scheduling Page

You can now customise the sequence of workcenters in the scheduling page to make it easier to track work in sequence or look at the most heavily loaded workcenters first.

07/2025
RFQ Module is Launched!

New RFQ module works from MRP, BOM and parts list to raise an RFQ for any part. Invite multiple suppliers, direct email and then convert the winning supplier to a PO as we automatically close the other RFQs.

07/2025
Intouch Update for End of Shift for Plastic Injection Moulders

For users of Intouch Monitoring, you can now update the quantities completed during a shift by imported the update .csv file from your Intouch system.

06/2025
Price lists and Price Break Quantities

The bill of materials (BOM) record now has an updated price list feature to allow for price break quantities to be created based on the pricing model you use.

12/2024
Drag and Drop Scheduling Gantt Chart

Major update to our scheduling tool - using a drag and drop gantt chart to plan your production schedule. Automatic scheduling puts all works order into the schedule starting at the current date using the earliest due date works order to start the sequence.

01/2021
Fraction ERP System is Launched!

The start of our product launch was functional and basic. At the time we thought it was pretty good, but we now love to look back and see how far we have come and think about how far we can take it!

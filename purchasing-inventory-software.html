Frequently Asked Questions

What is purchasing and inventory management in ERP?

It is the process of managing materials, suppliers, stock levels, and procurement — all connected to production demand.

How does MRP improve purchasing?

MRP calculates what materials are required based on production needs, ensuring accurate and timely purchasing decisions.

Can purchasing be automated in ERP?

Yes — purchase orders can be generated based on demand, stock levels, and production requirements.

How does ERP reduce inventory costs?

By aligning purchasing with real demand, ERP reduces excess stock, shortages, and inefficient buying.

What is SOP Software?

A Standard Operating Procedure (SOP) is a documented set of instructions that describes how a specific task should be performed — consistently, safely and to the right quality. SOP software replaces paper folders, network drives full of Word documents and PDF binders with a single, controlled, searchable system.

A modern SOP system should give you:

  • One controlled version of every procedure, accessible to the right people
  • A clear audit trail of who wrote, approved and read each revision
  • Training records that prove your team has been signed off on the latest version
  • Mobile-friendly access from the shop floor, not just the office

Done well, SOP software stops being "paperwork for the auditor" and starts being how your team actually works.

Why Paper and PDF SOPs Fail

Most manufacturers still rely on paper folders, shared drives or static PDFs for their procedures. The problems show up fast:

Old Revisions Stay in Use

Operators work from outdated copies because nobody knows which version is current.

No Proof of Training

No record of who has read or been trained on which version of which procedure.

Audit-Day Panic

Hours wasted printing, signing and back-filling records before every ISO 9001 audit.

Not Where the Work Happens

SOPs locked in an office binder are useless to operators on the shop floor.

Text-Only, No Visuals

A wall of text is a poor way to teach a hands-on task. Photos and videos are missing.

No Re-Training Trigger

When a procedure changes, there's no system to alert affected staff and re-collect sign-offs.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is SOP software?

SOP software replaces paper procedures and PDF binders with a controlled, searchable system. It manages document approvals and revisions, assigns training to users, captures their sign-off and gives you an audit trail you can show an ISO 9001 assessor.

How does Fraction ERP track SOP training?

A manager assigns an SOP to specific users. Each user signs off "I have read and understood Rev N" with one click; that record is locked to that exact revision. When the SOP is re-approved with material changes, the manager can flip everyone back to "re-training required" in one step.

How are SOP revisions managed?

Every approval freezes a snapshot of the SOP — content, steps, files and a required change summary. Revision numbers only bump on re-approval, so the version that was in force on any historical date can always be retrieved.

Does Fraction ERP support ISO 9001 quality management?

Yes. The SOP module covers documented information (clause 7.5) and competence records (clause 7.2). Combined with non-conformance reports, calibration logs and traceability, Fraction ERP gives you a single connected quality system instead of a stack of disconnected tools.

Can shop floor operators access SOPs on a tablet?

Yes. Every employee — including unlimited free shop floor data capture (SFDC) users — can open their personal "My SOPs" page on a phone or tablet, read the procedure with embedded photos and videos, and sign off without needing a full ERP licence.

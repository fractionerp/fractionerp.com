Embracing a cloud ERP system can be a game-changer for manufacturing and operations managers. If you're looking for ways to simplify your processes, boost productivity, and gain real-time control over your business, Fraction ERP's suite of features is designed to make your life easier.

## Why Choose Cloud ERP?

Cloud ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) solutions allow you to access your business data and tools from anywhere, at any time. No more being tied to the office or worrying about software updates—everything runs securely online, giving you flexibility and peace of mind.

## Key Fraction ERP Features That Make a Difference

### 1. RFQ Tool: Streamline Your Purchasing

- **Save time** by sending and comparing supplier quotes in one place
- **Improve accuracy** with digital records, reducing manual errors
- **Speed up decisions** by having all your RFQ data at your fingertips

### 2. Automatic Scheduling: Take the Guesswork Out of Planning

- **Automate production schedules** based on real-time data and priorities
- **React quickly** to changes with instant updates and notifications
- **Reduce bottlenecks** and keep your team focused on what matters

### 3. Interactive Dashboard: See the Big Picture Instantly

- **Monitor key metrics** like order progress, inventory, and deadlines
- **Spot issues early** with clear visual alerts and real-time updates
- **Make informed decisions** using live data, not outdated spreadsheets

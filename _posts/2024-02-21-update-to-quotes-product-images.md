We've just made an update to Fraction ERP that now allows you to include photos / images of your products on your quotes.


This small change is just one of the improvements we've made over the past couple of months.

The other changes in this release include:


- Increased accuracy on operation times (down to 0.1 seconds).
- Purchase orders can now include vendor part numbers.
- Simplification of the printing of labels for packing boxes.

When it comes to ERP systems, one of the biggest challenges for manufacturing and engineering businesses is getting up to speed quickly. Traditional ERP platforms are often packed with features, complex menus, and steep learning curves. This complexity can slow down onboarding, frustrate teams, and delay the benefits that a good ERP should deliver.

At Fraction ERP, we've taken a different approach with our V3 release: we've deliberately simplified the user interface to make learning and using the system as straightforward as possible.

## Why Simplicity Matters in ERP

- **Faster Onboarding:** A clean, intuitive interface means new users can find what they need without lengthy training sessions or manuals.

- **Reduced Errors:** When screens are uncluttered and navigation is logical, there's less chance of clicking the wrong button or misinterpreting data.

- **Confidence from Day One:** Users feel more comfortable experimenting and learning by doing, rather than worrying they'll break something.

- **Time Savings:** Less time spent hunting for features or second-guessing processes means more time focusing on real work.

## The Fraction ERP V3 Difference

We've listened to feedback from manufacturers and engineers who told us that time is their most precious resource. With Fraction ERP V3, we:

- **Streamlined Menus:** Everything you need is no more than a couple of clicks away. No more digging through endless submenus.

- **Consistent Layouts:** Each screen follows the same logic, so once you've learned one area, the rest feels familiar.

- **Clear Visuals:** We use simple, modern visuals and clear labels to avoid information overload.

- **Guided Actions:** Helpful prompts and tooltips support users as they go, making self-learning easy.

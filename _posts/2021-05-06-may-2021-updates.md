**DIRECT PDF PRINTING**
All quotes, order acknowledgements, purchase orders, invoices and delivery notes download to PDF.

**NEW EXCEL REPORTS**
New excel export reports added to purchase orders and invoices.

**CONTRACT DATE**
When converting a quote to sales order, the contract date is automatically set and can also be edited.

**INVENTORY REPORT**
On the parts page you can now download an inventory report to excel.

**DATE FILTER AND TOTAL**
All modules now feature date filters and a total value at the top the page.

**DELIVERY NOTE STATEMENT**
A custom title and text can be added to your delivery notes for statement of conformity or ownership clauses etc.

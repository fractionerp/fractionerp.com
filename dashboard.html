Dashboard

Take advantage of exception reporting and monitoring with our real time dashboard

Monitor business performance at a glance

With our clean dashboard you can see how your business is doing in just a few seconds. Whether you want to focus on stock issues, quality, sales or operations, our dashboard is your ally. Make the right decisions quickly with live, accurate, data from the heart of your business.

Speed up decision making

Monitor exceptions in real time with our quick links. The dashboard has business critical data presented when you open up Fraction ERP. Each dashboard tile has a link to let you drill down to where the decisions need to be made. From outstanding quotes to overdue purchase orders to late manufacturing operations. Click the link and view the items that need management attention.

Drive your daily production meetings

Monitor exceptions in real time with our quick links. The dashboard has business critical data presented when you open up Fraction ERP. Fraction ERP's dashboard can be used to drive daily production meetings. Commonly known as 'Sunrise Meetings', short meetings like this help to drive up productivity and performance. We've designed our dashboard to help with the daily roll call of the important tasks your business needs to manage.

Access anywhere, anytime with cloud ERP

Being cloud based, Fraction ERP allows you to access your data any time, any place from any device with a web browser. For senior management, this means that you can be out of the office and not be disconnected from the day-to-day activities taking place in your business.

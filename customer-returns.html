Customer Returns, Return to Vendor & Material Return Authorisation

Manage customer returns, supplier returns, RTVs and material return authorisations in one traceable ERP workflow.

Control customer returns from request to resolution

Capture returned goods, record the reason for return and keep the process visible from the moment a customer raises an issue. Fraction ERP helps your team manage return status, actions and follow-up without losing information in emails or spreadsheets.

Return faulty or incorrect materials to vendors

Use return to vendor workflows to manage supplier returns when purchased parts are damaged, incorrect or fail inspection. Keep the return linked to the supplier, part and purchasing record so your buying team has the full context.

Material return authorisation for controlled stock movement

Create a clear material return authorisation process before stock is moved, replaced or credited. This gives stores, purchasing and quality teams a shared record of what is being returned, why it is being returned and what needs to happen next.

Trace returns back to batches, serials and shipments

Connect returns to the original parts, batches, serial numbers and customer shipments where traceability is required. This makes it easier to investigate issues, isolate affected stock and support audit requirements.

Link returns to quality actions when required

When a return points to a wider quality issue, Fraction ERP can support the next step through non-conformance reporting and corrective action tracking. Returns stay connected to the operational and quality records around them.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is production scheduling in manufacturing?

Production scheduling is the process of planning when and how manufacturing operations are carried out to maximise efficiency and meet delivery deadlines.

What is the difference between planning and scheduling?

Planning defines what needs to be produced. Scheduling defines when and how it will be produced.

What is forward vs backward scheduling?

Forward scheduling plans from the earliest start date. Backward scheduling plans from the required completion date.

How does ERP improve production scheduling?

ERP connects scheduling with real-time data — including orders, inventory, and capacity — making schedules accurate and dynamic.

Production Scheduling Software

Unlock your productivity with our production scheduling and capacity management software for small and medium size manufacturers.

Easy to Use Scheduling Tools with Weekly and Daily Capacity Management

In Fraction ERP we developed a simple visual scheduling tool. See weekly or daily capacity buckets with a single glance. Use our auto planning tool or manually drag and drop operations to create your own optimal schedule.

Fraction is designed to auto schedule your production. You can launch one order at a time, refine it and then repeat. Or, you can load every order onto your scheduling board in one go.

Do less scheduling and get more product out the door!

Track Works Orders Through Production

Save hours every week and stop walking around the shop to monitor and track progress!

Use our real time works order tracking page to monitor progress on every order and quickly respond to any issues.

Quickly open an order to see current status and planning. Any updates are instantly updated into the production schedule.

Capacity Planning by Workcentres and Operators

Review your demand levels with our colour coded capacity board. Overloads are highlighted instantly. Flex and adjust your capacities if you can, or re-schedule orders with just a few clicks.

Fraction ERP allows you to allocate operations by workcentre, operators or both! This unique tool allows multiple constraints to be applied to planning simultaneously.

Take the guesswork out of your bottlenecks and instead drive up your utilisation and on time delivery performance.

Create Instant Work To Lists

By scheduling your work orders, Fraction ERP automatically generates 'work to lists'.

These lists instruct your teams what operations need to be completed when. This approach can improve on time delivery performance and labour utilisation. It also saves significant management and administration time.

Your team members can log onto the operations using our free SFDC (Shop Floor Data Capture) system, helping you track your production orders.

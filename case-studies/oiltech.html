The Challenge

A lean operation with no margin for admin overhead.

Oiltech Bearings is a small UK manufacturer supplying precision parts used in industrial rotating equipment — pumps, turbines and compressors. Their customers serve demanding industries including oil & gas, utilities, petrochemical and power generation.

As a make-to-order business managing a large and growing parts register, lead times are compressed — typically a few weeks, with emergency work sometimes needed within days.

With no office-based administrative staff, every process — sales, purchasing, production, shipping, and finance — had to run without adding overhead. The team needed a single system that connected every part of the business, accessible from anywhere, without the cost or complexity of traditional enterprise ERP.

The Solution

One system, built for how Oiltech actually works.

Quick Response to Enquiries

BOM copy and template tools let Oiltech cost-estimate and plan production at the enquiry stage — so when an order lands, they're already halfway through execution.

Nothing Falls Through the Cracks

Visual dashboards and stock alerts make sure no part — down to the smallest fastener — delays a complex assembly shipment.

Dual-Constraint Scheduling

Production is scheduled against both workcentre and operator capacity simultaneously — essential when specific skills are needed to hit delivery dates.

Fully Integrated, End to End

Sales through to invoicing runs in one system, with a Xero integration for accounting — no duplicated work, no reconciliation overhead.

Fraction has integrated all work processes so putting quotes into production is ridiculously quick and easy. Accurate stock inventory and managing the part demand now means nothing is overlooked, down to the smallest screw or pin.

Patrick Chester — Managing Director, Oiltech Bearings

Cloud Reliability & Mobility

Running a factory from anywhere.

As a small team with international clients, Oiltech's staff often spend significant time overseas. With Fraction ERP in the cloud, every member of the team works together regardless of location — and tablets on the shop floor keep works orders and drawings up to date in real time.

No on-premise servers. No upfront infrastructure cost. Just reliable access to the business from anywhere with an internet connection.

  • Raising purchase orders from demand is almost instant
  • Overdue purchases monitored to protect on-time delivery
  • Fast data interrogation and drill-down on any record
  • Concurrent design and production orders
  • Reduction in typos and manual errors

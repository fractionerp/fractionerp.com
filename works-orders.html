Works Orders

Organise your production with our easy-to-use works order management system

Manage demand, at a glance

Review what needs to be made, and when, with our part demand screen. No more guesswork required!

Track the status of your works orders

From awaiting planning, to in production and overdue. With just a couple of clicks you can manage the high-level flow of your production orders.

Make to order / make to stock

Create works orders directly from sales order demand or create works orders to replenish stock levels. However you want to control production, Fraction ERP is here.

Online or printed route cards

Choose between using your shop floor PCs or a traditional paper works order traveller. Use Fraction ERP the way that suits you best.

Online drawing and file storage

Add files and other information to your works orders to provide the right information at the right time for your production teams.

Modify works orders during production

Ideal for jobbing and service shops. If you need to tweak an order mid-way through production, no worries. Edit the works order and you're back on track.

Serialised traceability function

Available for each works order, you can add serial numbers to each production order. If you need traceability for your customer requirements, Fraction ERP is here to help.

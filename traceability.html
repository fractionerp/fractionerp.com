Traceability

Complete serial and batch traceability for all manufactured and bought in parts

Serial Number Tracking

Track individual items with unique serial numbers throughout your entire manufacturing process. From goods received through production and shipping, maintain complete visibility of every serialised part.

Batch and Lot Traceability

Manage batch and lot controlled inventory with full traceability. Record batch numbers at goods received, track through production and maintain records for complete audit trails.

Supplier Traceability

Link purchased parts to supplier batch and lot numbers. When quality issues arise, quickly identify affected stock and trace back to the source.

Production Traceability

Track which batches and serial numbers were used in production. Know exactly which components went into each finished product for complete forward and backward traceability.

Customer Shipment Records

Record which serial numbers and batches were shipped to each customer. Respond quickly to customer enquiries and manage any recalls efficiently.

Supports Compliance Requirements

Meet the traceability requirements of ISO 9001 and industry-specific standards. Maintain the documented records needed to demonstrate a fully traceable supply chain.

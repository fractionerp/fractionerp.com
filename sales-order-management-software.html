Why Most Sales Order Systems Fail in Manufacturing

Traditional sales order systems weren't built for manufacturing environments. They create disconnects between what is sold and what can actually be delivered.

  • Sales teams promise unrealistic delivery dates
  • Orders are not linked to BOMs or production routing
  • Production teams re-key or reinterpret order data
  • No real-time visibility once an order is released

The result: delays, errors, missed deadlines, and reduced margins.

Fraction ERP solves this by connecting sales directly to your manufacturing operations.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is sales order management in manufacturing?

Sales order management in manufacturing is the process of managing customer orders from quote through to production, delivery, and invoicing — ensuring all stages are connected and accurate.

How does ERP improve order processing?

An ERP system connects sales orders directly to production, inventory, and purchasing, eliminating manual work and providing real-time visibility.

What is quote-to-cash in manufacturing?

Quote-to-cash refers to the full lifecycle from creating a customer quote to receiving payment, including order processing, production, delivery, and invoicing.

Can sales orders be linked to production automatically?

Yes — in Fraction ERP, sales orders automatically generate works orders, material requirements, and purchasing actions.

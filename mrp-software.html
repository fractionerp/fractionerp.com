Frequently Asked Questions

What is manufacturing ERP software?

Manufacturing ERP software is a system that connects all business processes — including production, inventory, purchasing, and sales — into one platform.

How is manufacturing ERP different from standard ERP?

Manufacturing ERP includes specialised features such as MRP, production scheduling, and shop floor tracking.

Is ERP suitable for small manufacturers?

Yes — modern ERP systems are designed to scale and can be implemented in growing manufacturing businesses.

How long does ERP implementation take?

Implementation time varies, but modern cloud ERP systems are significantly faster to deploy than traditional systems.

What is MRP (Material Requirements Planning)?

MRP is a method used to calculate:

  • What materials are required
  • How much is needed
  • When they are needed

It works by combining:

  • Sales demand
  • Bill of materials (BOM)
  • Current inventory levels

The goal: ensure materials are available for production at the right time, without holding unnecessary stock.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is MRP software?

MRP software automates the planning of materials required for production, ensuring availability while minimising excess inventory.

What is the difference between MRP and ERP?

MRP focuses on materials planning. ERP includes MRP plus production, inventory, purchasing, and business processes in one system.

Can MRP work in spreadsheets?

It can, but it becomes complex, error-prone, and difficult to scale as your business grows. Read more about MRP in Excel.

How does ERP improve MRP?

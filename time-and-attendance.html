The problem with separate systems

“Do I have enough people on the floor today?” should not be hard to answer.

Manufacturers often juggle three or four disconnected tools to answer that one question. A clocking machine says who entered the building. An HR spreadsheet says who booked holiday. A scheduling tool — updated manually — says who might be assigned where.

None of them talk to each other, and by the time someone notices a gap, production is already behind. Fraction ERP closes that gap by making attendance, leave and scheduling one connected workflow rather than three systems that need reconciling.

Frequently asked questions

Time & Attendance questions

Does Time & Attendance work with our existing production scheduling?

Yes. It is built directly into Fraction ERP’s scheduling tool, so approved leave and absence update employee availability without manual input.

Can employees access it from their phones?

Yes. Employees can view entitlement, leave history and available days, request leave, and clock in or out through the mobile app.

Do we need individual logins for shop floor clocking?

No. Kiosk mode supports clock-in and clock-out from a shared device without an individual account login.

How does geofencing work?

Mobile clocking is restricted to a defined radius around each site, so employees can only clock in or out when physically on site.

What happens if someone leaves a job open?

The employee must close all active shop floor data collection logs before the system allows them to clock out.

What is Bradford Factor scoring?

It is a formula that highlights absence patterns by weighting frequent short absences more heavily than occasional longer ones. Fraction ERP calculates the score automatically for each employee.

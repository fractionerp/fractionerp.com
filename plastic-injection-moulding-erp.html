Why Traditional ERP Systems Fall Short for Injection Moulding

Most ERP systems are designed to manage:

  • Inventory
  • Sales orders
  • Purchasing

But injection moulding companies operate in a much more complex environment.

  • No visibility of what's happening on the shop floor
  • Production updates delayed or inaccurate
  • Scrap and waste not properly tracked
  • Mould tools managed outside the ERP
  • Machine performance not monitored

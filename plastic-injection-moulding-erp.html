Why Traditional ERP Systems Fall Short

Most ERP systems are built to manage business processes like:

  • Sales orders
  • Inventory
  • Purchasing

But injection moulding companies need much more than that. Without real-time visibility of production, manufacturers often struggle with:

  • Limited insight into what is happening on the shop floor
  • Delayed or inaccurate production updates
  • Poor visibility of scrap and material waste
  • Mould tools managed outside the ERP system
  • No clear view of machine performance or utilisation

The result? Missed inefficiencies, higher costs, and reduced profitability.

Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

What size of business is this most relevant for?

Typically businesses with 5–100 staff running manufacturing or similar operations. If you're smaller or larger, it's still worth a conversation — we'll tell you if it is not the right fit.

What happens after the call?

Nothing, unless you want it to. If a demo makes sense, we'll suggest it, but you're in control of what happens next.

Can I bring someone else from my team?

Yes. If your operations manager, finance lead, or another decision-maker wants to join, that is fine. It can be useful to have more than one perspective in the room.

What should I bring to the call?

Come ready to talk about how you currently manage orders, purchasing, stock, production, and reporting. You do not need a formal brief.

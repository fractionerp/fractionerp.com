The Problem

If Any of These Sound Familiar, You've Probably Outgrown Your Spreadsheets

01

Version Chaos

Multiple copies of the same spreadsheet floating around. Nobody knows which one is current — until a wrong version ships a bad order.

02

Manual Re-keying

The same data typed into three different sheets. Sales, production, and purchasing each maintain their own version of the truth.

03

Stock Guesswork

You think you have 200 units. The shelf says 140. The spreadsheet hasn't been updated since last Tuesday.

04

Quoting Takes Hours

Every quote requires pulling data from multiple sheets, cross-referencing costs, and hoping the formulas haven't broken.

05

No Production Visibility

What's on the shop floor right now? What's overdue? The spreadsheet can't tell you — because nobody's updated it.

06

Growth Hits a Wall

You've got more orders than ever. But the spreadsheet process that worked for 10 orders a week breaks at 50.

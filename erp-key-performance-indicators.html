Key Performance Indicators (KPIs)

Key performance indicators show you the trends and analysis needed to track performance and measure your results. Using data is critical in decision making. Fraction ERP makes understanding what is happening in your business so much easier.

Invoices Sales

Track your sales output by week or month and filter by customer name.

Order Intake

Monitor sales order intake by week or month, and filter by customer name. This allows you to understand trends in your order book and allow you to respond to changing markets.

On Time Delivery

Track your on time delivery performance to identify improvements you can make to become the supplier of choice for your customers. Review data by customer and specific time periods.
